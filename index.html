Welcome aboard, I'm Srishti Singh

Hey there! I'm Srishti, a strategic data professional with a twist of adventure. After 3.5 years of immersive experience in Data Analytics, I decided to take a leap of faith and journeyed from the vibrant streets of Bengaluru, India, to the bustling tech hub of Boston, USA. Driven by a passion for uncovering insights through data, I earned a Bachelor's in Information & Technology and honed my skills in the industry.
